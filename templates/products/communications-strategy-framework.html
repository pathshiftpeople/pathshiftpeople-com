Courses at a glance

Who are these courses for?

If you've ever been tasked with the development of your organisation's communication strategy and been bewildered and perplexed by the sheer variety of communications tactics out there, this course is for you.

At the heart of all of these courses is a simple framework which separates communication strategy from communications tactics, allowing you to define the essence of your organisation's communication strategy with just a few interconnected tables. Depending on the course you take, you'll then use this approach to develop either the executive summary of the communications strategy, of a much more complete version.

No prior knowledge is required.
