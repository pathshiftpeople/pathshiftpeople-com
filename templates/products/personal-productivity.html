Preview: Personal Productivity Courses

Whatever your productivity system, these short courses will help you improve it.

In every course, the goal is to help you develop a personal productivity system that meets your unique needs, rather than training you to adopt someone else's, and ensuring that the resulting system saves you more time than you actually spend developing and refining it.

No prior knowledge is required, and most courses are technology-agnostic (exception: Personal Productivity & Obsidian), although the concrete examples will by definition demonstrate the specific tools we use in our personal productivity systems.
