Wendy Elford

Dr Wendy Elford is a 'practical futurist' with a penchant for design strategy and knowledge design. She's committed to cultivating sustainable systems within the realms of health, education, businesses, and communities, while maintaining a focus on inclusive, human-centric, and user-centred design. Wendy teams up with other designers and business leaders to maintain a holistic view of the product context, tracking real-time outcomes from prototype to final utilization scenarios.

Her unique approach is rooted in complex adaptive systems, using text, large language models, observation, and narratives as data to inform the design process. Frequently Asked Questions

Why PathShiftPeople?

PathShiftPeople's founders, like most people we know, have already had to "shift path" several times to date.

Sometimes shifting path means taking a new direction in your professional life, sometimes it's more personal. Sometimes it's your choice, sometimes it's imposed. But whatever the circumstances, shift happens.

And with the arrival of Large Language Models today, and AGI sometime down the road, it'll happen more and more frequently.

When the one constant is change, when the ground never stops moving under your feet, only one essential skill remains: navigating path shifts. So we decided to create something to help people prepare.

How do you deliver your courses

You don't need any software, as we deliver most of the above courses via Gumroad (visit our store), which also handles the purchasing processes. However, as many of our courses combine "on demand" content (texts, videos and downloadables) with webinars, where you can ask questions and exchange ideas. We manage these with Calendly and hold them on Zoom.

What happens if I miss a lesson? Or three?

If you're taking a course with one or more live meetings, missing is a shame, but not the end of the world:

  • all live meetings should be recorded, if all participants' consent can be obtained, so you should be able to watch the meeting later
  • in some circumstances we can give you credit to join the lesson in a subsequent course.

In all cases, just talk to your course leader.

Do you offer bundles?

Not yet, but many courses include discount coupons for related courses, effectively giving you both the bundle discount and "try before you buy".

Can I purchase a gift course or bundle for a friend?

Not yet, but we are working on it. Please contact us and we will set it up for you.

How do I become a PathShiftPerson?

So you'd like to appear under About Us, above? We're currently developing a process, but for now simply email us outlining how you believe you can bring value to our community, and we will get back to you (see How do I contact you?, below).

How do I contact you?

If you are taking a course you can reach out to the teacher via the community as well as during any online workshops and webinars.

For all other questions, please email support@pathshiftpeople.com.
